lightbulb How It Works

Welcome to the Smart Warehouse Space Optimization System! This tool helps you figure out the most profitable or important items to store when you have limited warehouse space.

1

Set Capacity

Start by telling us the maximum weight (or volume) your warehouse can handle.

2

Add Items

List all the potential items you could store. Give each item a weight and a Value Score (think of value as priority, profit, or importance).

3

Optimize

Click "Run Optimization". Under the hood, we use the famous 0/1 Knapsack algorithm to automatically select a combination of items that maximizes the total value without exceeding your capacity constraints!

Pro Tip: If two items have the same weight and value, the algorithm will break the tie by choosing essential categories like Food and Medicine first!
